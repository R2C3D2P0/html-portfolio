The best movies according to Amy Rolfe

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Lord of the Rings The Two Towers

The acting in this movie is incredible as well as the graphics. For example, the battle at the end of the movie looks so real. For me, its my favourite movie of all time.

Harry potter and the Deathly Hallows Part 2

This is in my top 3 as I grew up on harry potter the books and the movies. The last movie just made me emotional and it felt like my childhood was over when this movie ended. I had grown up with this series.

Me Before You

I enjoy a love story movie and this movie has it all. The romantic story telling with the emotional realism of what life has to offer. It did make me cry so bring tissues.
